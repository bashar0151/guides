**Debrid Vault (DV)** is a feature-rich media manager that integrates with multiple of your debrid cloud providers [(Real-Debrid and TorBox)](../debrid.md). 

DV acts as a central location to manage all your media (movies, shows, music, comics, audiobooks, books and games) in one place. It doesn't stop there—DV lets you search, stream, and organise your entire library and more. See some of the features below!

## What Features Does it Have?
* **Built-in media player**
* **Playlist creation:** Custom shareable playlists!
* **Metadata Scraper:** From iTunes, Spotify, and Musicbrainz!
* **Search:** Find music from various sources.
* **Full Screen & Miniplayer:** Versatile playback options.
* **Tab Navigation:** Easily switch between search, hub, and library.
* **Multi-select Actions:** Add to playlist, queue, or download to local storage.
* **Stats Page:** Review your listening habits.
